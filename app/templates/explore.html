Ants From Up There

Ants From Up There is UK-based Black Country, New Road’s second studio album, released February 4th, 2022 by Ninja Tune. Announced on October 12th in tandem with the release of lead single “Chaos Space Marine”, the band described the album as “sad, epic and possibly more universally likeable… possibly” in an interview with NME. A press release described the album as “[traversing] classical minimalism, indie-folk, pop, alt rock and a distinct tone that is already unique to the band”.
